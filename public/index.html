About Uma

Uma is a personal AI assistant built to answer clearly, stream responses line by line, and keep your conversations available in local history.

Ask about Java, REST APIs, portfolio ideas, debugging, project descriptions, interview prep, or anything you want to understand step by step.

How History Works

Your chat history is saved only in this browser through local storage. Use New chat to start fresh, or Clear to remove all saved conversations.

Privacy

Messages are sent to the local Uma server and then to Gemini for AI responses. Your sidebar history stays on your device unless you clear browser data.
